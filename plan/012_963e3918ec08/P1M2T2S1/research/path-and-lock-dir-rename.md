## 3. WHY there is NO overlap with the parallel S2 (git-config keys)

S2's perl pass is `s/(?<![.\/])stagehand\./stagecoach./g` ‚Äî it matches `stagehand` followed by `.` (a dot),
UNLESS preceded by `.` or `/`. This task's targets are structurally DIFFERENT and S2 provably does not touch them:
- `"stagehand"` (quoted, in `filepath.Join(‚Ä¶, "stagehand", ‚Ä¶)`) ‚Äî after `stagehand` comes `"` (closing quote),
  NOT `.` ‚Üí S2's `stagehand\.` does not match. ‚úì S2 leaves it; MINE.
- `.stagehand.toml` ‚Äî S2's lookbehind `(?<![.\/])` EXCLUDES it (preceded by `.`). ‚úì S2 preserves it; MINE.
- `stagehand/config.toml`, `stagehand/locks` (in doc comments) ‚Äî after `stagehand` comes `/`, NOT `.` ‚Üí no match. ‚úì MINE.
- `stagehand:` / `stagehand ` (error/notice strings) ‚Äî after `stagehand` comes `:` or space, NOT `.` ‚Üí no match. ‚úì MINE.

So after S2 lands, every target in ¬ß1 is INTACT, waiting for this task. Conversely, this task's edits (quoted
`"stagehand"`, `.stagehand.toml`, `stagehand/‚Ä¶`, `stagehand:`) do NOT match S2's `stagehand.` pattern ‚Üí this
task cannot touch S2's git-config keys. **The two passes are disjoint by construction.**
